Apply for study leave

You can use this service to apply for and manage study leave and to claim expenses

Before you start

To successfully make a study leave application, you’ll need:

  • details about the course or event you want to attend
  • agreement from your educational supervisor that the course is appropriate
  • permission from your rota coordinator to be away from work
  • the name and contact details of the person who’ll approve your application—this is usually your Training Programme Director (TPD)
  • estimated costs that will be incurred, for example course fees, travel and accommodation

Once you’ve attended the course

After you’ve attended the course, you can:

  • come back and claim the expenses you incurred

Permission to attend

You must have permission to attend the course from your educational supervisor and rota coordinator.
