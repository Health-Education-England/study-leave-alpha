Do you expect to incur any other expenses?

Page 3 of 4

As part of your application, you need to provide estimates for any expenses you expect to incur.

What can I claim expenses for?

You can request reimbursement for:

  • travel costs to get to a study leave event
  • course fees to attend the event
  • accommodation costs if the event requires an overnight stay, up to £120 per night (or £150 in London)

You'll automatically be issued a daily amount for subsistence depending on the duration of the study leave event.
